# Managed Services Retainer Agreement - Example MSP

## 1. Scope of Services
MSP will provide ongoing managed IT support, monitoring, patching, helpdesk, and infrastructure management services as outlined in the attached Service Catalog. All services reasonably requested by Client in connection with supported environments are included within the monthly retainer fee. MSP shall provide additional support, projects, and on-site assistance as needed without additional billing unless pre-approved in writing.

## 2. Payment and Billing
Client agrees to pay the fixed monthly retainer of $8,500 on Net 45 terms. Invoices are due upon receipt of monthly performance report. MSP may withhold service if any invoice remains unpaid beyond 45 days at MSP's discretion.

## 3. Term, Renewal and Fees
The initial term is six (6) months. This Agreement automatically renews for additional six-month periods at MSP's then-current published rates unless Client provides written notice of intent not to renew at least fifteen (15) days before expiration. Pricing for renewal periods may be adjusted upward by MSP with thirty days notice.

## 4. IP and Work Product
All scripts, monitoring configurations, documentation, runbooks, and tools developed or customized during the engagement are work for hire and become Client property immediately. MSP assigns all intellectual property rights without limitation. MSP may not use any developed materials, even generalized versions, for any other client.

## 5. Audit and Compliance Rights
Client shall have unlimited audit rights over MSP's security controls, personnel, and systems. MSP must provide access within 48 hours of any request and accommodate on-site visits by Client or its auditors at MSP's expense. MSP must maintain all records for seven years.

## 6. Termination and Exit
Either party may terminate for convenience with 30 days notice. Upon termination, MSP will provide reasonable transition assistance at standard hourly rates. Client may request full pro-rata refund of the current month retainer.

## 7. Remedies and Credits
If MSP fails to meet any SLA target in a calendar month, Client is entitled to a credit of 150% of the monthly retainer fee for that month plus reimbursement for any third-party costs incurred due to the outage.

## 8. Data Handling
MSP will not access Client data except as necessary. Upon request or termination, MSP will securely delete or return all Client data and provide written certification within 5 business days. Client reserves broad rights to conduct forensic audits post-termination.
