# Better Terms Agreement

1. Services
Vendor will deliver the scope described in Exhibit A. Requests outside Exhibit A require a written change order.

2. Payment Terms
Customer will pay 50 percent at signature and the remainder on Net 15 terms. Deliverables are deemed accepted if Customer does not reject them in writing within five business days.

3. Renewal
The subscription renews annually unless either party gives 60 days notice before renewal. Termination for convenience is not available during an active annual term.

4. Service Credits
Service credits are Customer's sole remedy for verified SLA misses and are capped at the fees paid for the affected month.
