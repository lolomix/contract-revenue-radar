# Revenue Protection Sprint

Fixed-scope package: **$5,000**

## Buyer

Implementation partners, RevOps firms, MSPs, ERP/CRM consultants, and professional-services teams with repeated SOWs, MSAs, order forms, renewal terms, or implementation agreements.

## Problem

Revenue gets delayed or leaked when risky terms are caught too late:

- payment after subjective acceptance,
- vague customer dependencies,
- included work with no change-order boundary,
- short termination or non-renewal windows,
- broad service credits or refund rights,
- unresolved data/security review obligations,
- broad IP assignment that blocks reuse of tools or methodologies.

## Scope

- Review 15-25 redacted documents, templates, or excerpts.
- Run Contract Revenue Radar and perform business-risk review.
- Produce a prioritized risk report.
- Create fallback positions for sales/ops negotiation.
- Create a reusable clause playbook.
- Create an intake checklist before legal review.
- Include a 60-minute review session.

## Deliverables

- Markdown/PDF report.
- Clause playbook.
- Sales/ops intake checklist.
- Optional API/tool-call demo for internal automation teams.

## Limit

This is business-risk review, not legal advice. Client counsel approves final contract language.
