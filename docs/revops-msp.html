Sample findings

Payment timing
Payment is due Net 60 after Customer acceptance of the monthly services report.

Fallback: invoice-date payment, Net 15/30, milestone billing, or deemed acceptance after a fixed review window.

Support scope
Vendor will provide reasonable support and all assistance required for launch.

Fallback: included hours, named support channels, change-order triggers, and out-of-scope rates.

Reusable IP
All deliverables and work product are assigned to Customer.

Fallback: retain background IP, methodologies, tools, and generic components while licensing client-specific deliverables.
