Sample findings public-style excerpt
Payment delay 5/5
Payment is Net 60 after acceptance of the monthly service summary.

Fallback: Net 15/30 from invoice date, milestone billing, or deemed acceptance after a fixed review window.

Scope creep 4/5
Managed-service requests are included as reasonably requested by Customer.

Fallback: define included hours, change-order triggers, revision limits, and out-of-scope rates.

Renewal loss 4/5
The engagement renews month-to-month and may be terminated with 15 days notice.

Fallback: annual renewal, 60-90 day non-renewal notice, or a transition fee for early exit.
