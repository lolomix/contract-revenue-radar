What Approval Covers

  • Business-risk review for sales, finance, delivery, operations, and counsel coordination.
  • Review of redacted SOWs, MSAs, order forms, retainers, or recurring service templates.
  • Exact excerpts, risk severity, business impact, priority questions, and fallback positions.
  • Private document intake only after package, approval route, and payment/procurement path are confirmed.

Acceptance Criteria

  • Selected package and document count are confirmed.
  • Payment, funded platform order, or accepted written payment/procurement terms are confirmed.
  • Redacted files or excerpts are received through an agreed private route.
  • One business owner is available for questions and review-call scheduling.
